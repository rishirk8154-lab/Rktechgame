Explore Aurangabad - Tour Package

Aurangabad, Maharashtra is rich in heritage and natural beauty. Explore these top tourist attractions with us during your stay.

Ajanta Caves

UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring 30 rock-cut Buddhist cave monuments adorned with ancient murals and sculptures dating back to 2nd century BC. A must-visit for history and art enthusiasts.

Ellora Caves

Another UNESCO site comprising 34 caves representing Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain rock-cut temples, showcasing Indiaâ€™s rich religious history and architectural excellence.

Bibi Ka Maqbara

Often called the 'Mini Taj', this mausoleum built in the 1660s by Aurangzeb's son commemorates his wife. The structure is famous for its resemblance to the Taj Mahal and beautiful gardens.

Daulatabad Fort

A 14th-century fortress known for its complex defenses and commanding views, offering history lovers a glimpse into medieval Indian fort architecture and strategic military design.

Aurangabad Caves

A set of 12 rock-cut Buddhist shrines famed for their sculptures and murals depicting Vajrayana Buddhism. These caves provide a serene atmosphere combined with rich historical significance.
