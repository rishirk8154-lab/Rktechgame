Welcome to Hotel Avon International

Hotel Avon International is a value for money hotel. The rooms are of 5 star quality, which are offered at reasonable rates.

All rooms are equipped with LED T.V.’s along with DTH, Voltas split a.c. Many rooms have 9” comfortable coire mattress & latest furniture range in the rooms.

Comfort and care of guests are the main objectives of Hotel Avon and the rooms rightly reflect this attitude. The Hotel Avon International in Aurangabad captures special attention among the entire 3 star Luxury Hotels.
