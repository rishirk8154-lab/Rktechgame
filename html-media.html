HTML me Embedded Media

Webpages me media content jaise audio, video, PDF ya doosre webpages ko embed karna important hota hai. HTML5 ne iske liye kai tags provide kiye hain jinse ye kaafi asaan ho gaya hai.

<video> Tag

HTML5 me video files embed karne ke liye <video> tag ka use hota hai. Isme aap multiple source de sakte hain aur controls automatic milte hain.

<video width="480" controls>
  <source src="flower.webm" type="video/webm">
  <source src="flower.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>

<audio> Tag

Audio files ko embed karne ke liye <audio> tag ka use hota hai. Isme bhi multiple sources aur controls hota hai.

<audio controls>
  <source src="roar.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
  Your browser does not support the audio element.
</audio>

<embed> Tag

<embed> tag se aap external content embed kar sakte hain jaise PDF, Flash, ya koi bhi plugin-based content.

<embed src="document.pdf" type="application/pdf" width="100%" height="400px" />

<iframe> Tag

Iframe aapko ek dusre webpage ko apne webpage ke andar dikhane ka moka deta hai.

<iframe src="https://www.google.com" width="600" height="400" title="Google"></iframe>

Summary

HTML5 ke ye tags embedded media ko asani se webpage me dikhane ka tarika dete hain, bina kisi third-party plugin ke zarurat ke. Video, audio, documents, ya external pages sab asani se embed kiye ja sakte hain.
